## Late Fee

Late payment charges are charged to your credit cards when minimum payments aren't received by due dates. Review our fee structure and requirements.

### Important information

- Setting up alerts in your Smart Phone's Calendar can save you from late payments charges!
- Learn how to [Set up GIRO Payment for your Credit Card Bill](card-payment-giro-application.html).
- Learn how to [Pay Credit Card Bills Instantly](card-payment-pay-credit-card-bills.html).

### How to identify a late fee

There are various channels which you may identify late fee with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.
