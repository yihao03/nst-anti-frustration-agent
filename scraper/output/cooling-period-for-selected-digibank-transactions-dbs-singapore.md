## Cooling Period for selected digibank transactions

We're constantly enhancing our security measures to keep your account safe from the rising threat of scams.

### Important information

- A 12\-hour cooling period will be progressively implemented for the following selected digital banking services, such as:
	- Adding a new transfer recipient
	- Increasing daily local \& overseas transfer limits
	- Updating contact details (email, mobile number)
- With this 12\-hour cooling period, it gives you a window to detect and report any suspicious activity if anyone tries to make unauthorised changes to your account.
- You will be able to transfer funds to the new transfer recipient or up to your new transfer limits, only after the cooling period is over.
- For application of new banking products and services, you may wish to proceed after the cooling period is over so that updated contact and address details will be reflected.

### Here are some tips for seamless banking

###### Pre\-plan your transfers
If you anticipate needing to add a new transfer recipient, or increase your limits, do so at least a day in advance.
