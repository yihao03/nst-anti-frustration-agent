### **3\. Using DBS digibank provides added card security**

Barring any unforeseen circumstances, it takes 15 working days for your credit card application to be processed upon receipt of all required information and documents. Following that, you should receive your card in the mail within a few days. If you are not registered for the digibank mobile app, you will also receive a separate mail containing your preliminary credit card PIN (Personal Identification Number).

While mail in Singapore does not often get lost in transit, it can go missing once in a blue moon. This means it is still possible that your credit card or PIN mailer may not reach you. In the event this happens, the bank will take the precautionary action of deactivating your card to prevent misuse of it.

This can become a frustrating affair as it takes time to reactivate or apply for a new card.

By using digibank to activate your card, both you and the bank can be assured of whether the credit card/PIN has reached you, thus eliminating the risks and inconveniences associated with the loss of them.

### **4\. Start using your card to build your credit history**

One of the benefits of using a credit card is the opportunity to build your [personal credit history](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/articles/nav/budget-spend/debt-series-understanding-credit-scores?pid=sg-dbs-pweb-article-credit-card-activation-and-why-it-matters-txtlink-personal-credit-history).

When you apply for loans, financial institutions use your credit history to determine your credit worthiness as a borrower. This in turn affects whether your loan is approved, at what rate, and the loan amount.

Your credit history is built when you make transactions on your credit card, and then pay your bills in a timely manner and in full.

Without activating your credit card, you cannot start making purchases on it and thereby delay the opportunity to start building your credit score.
