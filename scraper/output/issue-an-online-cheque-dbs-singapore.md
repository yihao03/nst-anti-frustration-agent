## Issue an Online Cheque

Save on your cheque book fees! Simply issue an online cheque via digibank online.

Consider using [Electronic Deferred Payment (EDP)](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/deposits/pay-with-ease/electronic-deferred-payment?pid=sg-dbs-help-support-bank-cheque-purchase-ibcheque)1, a new, free service providing a streamlined digital alternative to cheques and cashier's orders.  
 Benefits of EDP: 

- Search for recipients via PayNow or account number
- Send or receive an EDP via digibank app instantly
- Easy and convenient tracking

*1Ensure your recipient has a valid account with EDP participating banks (Citibank, DBS Bank, HSBC, Maybank, OCBC Singapore, Standard Chartered Bank and UOB)* 

### Important information

- Online Cheque has a validity period of 2 months and can only be deposited into an account with a bank in Singapore.
- Online Cheques are mailed out at no charges to local addresses only.

### How to issue an online cheque

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

### More information

- The cheque amount will be refunded to the debiting account 7 days after the Online Cheque expires.
