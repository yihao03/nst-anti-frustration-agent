## Cards for Life: Credit Card Eligibility for 65 years old and above

Starting 11 June 2025, customers aged 65 and above can apply for any DBS/POSB credit card using CPF LIFE payouts as income proof.  
  

 You may apply in person at any of our DBS/POSB branches with the required documents or by calling our Customer Centre at 1800 111 1111, fill in the PDF application form, attach a copy of your NRIC and at least one income document and mail it in.

- If youâre 56 years old to 75 years old, youâll need a minimum annual income of S$15,000 for Singaporeans/ Permanent Residents.
- If youâre below 56 years of age, refer to [Eligibility for Credit Card Application](card-application-eligibility.html) for more information.
- Receive a **sign\-up gift** if;
	- You are currently not holding on to any Principal DBS/POSB Credit Card and;
	- Did not cancel your Principal DBS/POSB Credit Card within the last 12 months.
	- Be sure to use the applicable promo code in the application form. Refer to our current [promotions](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/cards/promotions/default.page) for more information.

- A copy of NRIC (Front \& Back); **and**
- At least one of the income documents below, please provide latest copy.
 

| Income | Document(s) required | Period (months/year) |
| --- | --- | --- |
| Rental | Income Tax Notice of Assessment in SGD | 1 year |
| Pension | For pension payout received in DBS/POSB accounts, please indicate your personal Savings/Current account number under the Income Information Submission section on the application form (available at our Branches or call our Customer Centre)  or  Personal Bank statement in SGD showing pension payout. | 3 months |
| Dividend or Interest income | Income Tax Notice of Assessment in SGD **and/ or**Annual personal bank statement in SGD showing Dividend or interest income | 1 year |
| CPF Life Payout | CPF monthly payouts history statement showing CPF Life payoutorPersonal Bank statement in SGD showing CPF Life payout. | 1 month |
| Retirement Sum Scheme (RSS) | CPF Transaction History showing RSS payout and RSS Balance | 1 year |
| SalaryEmployees with length of service more than 3 months | Computerized payslip in SGD or  Salary Crediting bank statements in SGD *For salary credited into DBS/POSB account, indicate your personal Savings/Current account number under the Income Information Submission section on the application form (available at our branches or call our customer centre)* | 3 months |
| SalaryEmployees with length of service less than 3 months | Latest computerised payslip or   Company letter certifying employment and salary in SGD (signed copy by HR and you) and a copy of Staff Pass | \- |
| Variable/ Commission\-based Employees/ Self\-Employed | Income Tax Notice of Assessment in SGD | 1 year (2 years preferred) |
- You can provide income document(s) that sum up to a minimum of S$1,250 monthly income ($15,000 annual income)
- We don't accept Assets Under Management (AUM) in place of income for unsecured credit card applications. However, you may consider applying for a Secured Credit Card by placing a Fixed Deposit. Learn more about [Secured cards](card-application-eligibility.html).
- Credit limit is determined at DBS' discretion. However, we will take your preferred limit into consideration and assign a credit limit not exceeding your preferred limit.

- You can apply in person at any of our DBS/POSB branches with the required documents or by calling our Customer Centre at 1800 111 1111\. Fill in the PDF application form and attach a copy of your NRIC and at least one income document and mail it in.
- Processing will take 7 to 9 working days after receiving the completed application with all the required documents.
	- Once approved, the Credit Card will be mailed to your mailing address.
	- If your application is declined, you will be notified by mail.
