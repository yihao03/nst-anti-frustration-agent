## Check Home Loan Interest for Tax Filing

Find out the total interest charged for last year on your home loan in your DBS/POSB December Consolidated Statement or eStatement via digibank online.

### Important information

- Tax filing applies only to **investment properties**.

### How to find out the total interest charged for last year

- Refer to either **December Consolidated Statement** or [eStatement via digibank online](bank-statements-viewing-estatements.html) and look for Total Interest Charged.
- If you have multiple sub\-loans, refer to both sub\-loan accounts in the December Consolidated statement or eStatement to add up the **Total Interest Charged** respectively.
- If any sub\-loan(s) was redeemed during the year, refer to the detailed statement of account sent after the closure of the sub\-loan(s).
