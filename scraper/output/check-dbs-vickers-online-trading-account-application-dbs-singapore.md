## Check DBS Vickers Online Trading Account Application Status

Check your DBS Vickers Online Trading Account Application Status via digibank online.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### How to check DBS Vickers Online Trading Account application status

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

### More information

- Check your email (also the junk/spam folder) to see if you received an email from DBS Vickers. There might be times that we would need to clarify with you some details on your application over email.
