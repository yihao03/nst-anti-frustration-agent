## Credit Card Payment Cut\-off Times

Perform instant payment for your DBS/POSB Credit Cards via digibank. Alternatively, other non\-instant channels includes Phone Banking, ATM, AXS, GIRO and Cheque.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Go Travel with DBS](guide-travel.html)

### Payment Cut\-off Times

  

| Payment Channel | Payment Crediting |
| --- | --- |
| [digibank Online](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/) \& digibank Mobile | - Instant crediting |
| Phone Banking/ ATM | - Next working day *(if payment is made before 11\.30pm from Mon\-Sat, \& before 8pm on the last working day of the month)* |
| AXS Stations | - Next working day *(if payment is made before 4\.55pm from Mon\-Fri)*. - 2nd working day *(if payment is made after 4\.55pm on Friday and on Weekends)*. |
| InterBank GIRO | - 3rd working day 	- [Set up a GIRO arrangement easily via digibank Online](card-payment-giro-application.html) |
| Quick Cheque Deposit | - 3rd working day, if the cheque is dropped before 1pm 	- Cheque payable to 'DBS\-Cards' 	- Card Account number(s) and the corresponding amount(s) payable for each Card Account indicated at the back of the cheque |
| Cheque Mail\-in | - 3rd working day after we receive the cheque 	- Cheque payable to 'DBS\-Cards' 	- Card Account number(s) and the corresponding amount(s) payable for each Card Account indicated at the back of the cheque 	- Mail in your cheque to: DBS CARDS, Orchard Road P.O. BOX 360, Singapore 912312 or download a copy of the [Business Reply Envelope](https://www.dbs.com.sg/iwov-resources/pdf/cards/business_reply_envelope.pdf) |
