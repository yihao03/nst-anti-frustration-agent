### How to update your Personal Details

There are various channels to update your personal particulars. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.


 There are several ways to update your profile: 
 - Retrieve info (e.g. name, birth date, nationality, foreigner's pass) from Singpass
- Upload your new ID (e.g. passport or foreigner pass)
- Manually input your latest info (e.g. marital status, employment info, CDP Account No)
