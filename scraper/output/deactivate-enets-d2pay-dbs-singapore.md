## Deactivate eNets (D2Pay)

Learn how to de\-activate your eNets (D2Pay) service easily using digibank.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to digibank](guide-ibanking.html)

### How to deactivate eNets (D2Pay)

There are various channels which you may deactivate eNets (D2Pay) with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

### More information

- The deactivation is immediate. No DBS d2pay payments will be allowed until you re\-activate this service in writing using the [DBS d2pay Multi\-Purpose Form](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/forms/paymentstransfers/d2payindividual/Pages/default.page).
