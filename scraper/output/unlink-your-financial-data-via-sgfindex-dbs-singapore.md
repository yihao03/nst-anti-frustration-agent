## Unlink your financial data via SGFinDex

Unlink your external accounts from SGFinDex easily with Singpass.

### Important information

- Government accounts from CPF, IRAS and HDB cannot be unlinked.

  

### How to unlink your financial data via SGFinDex

There are various channels which you may unlink your financial data via SGFinDex. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.
