## Manage Accounts Linked to your Card

Learn how you can easily link your card to your Savings or Current Account via digibank.

### How to link your Savings/Current account to your card

There are various channels which you may use to link your Savings or Current Account to your card with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

- Insert your **ATM/Debit/Credit Card** or scan your **NRIC** or **Passport** and complete the authentication process.
- Under **Card Services**, select **Change Account Linkage**.
- Choose your **Card** you wish to update.
- Select your **Primary(Main) Account** and **Secondary Accounts** to be linked.
- Get a **One Time Password via SMS** and key it into the Video Teller Machine (VTM).
- Press **Confirm** to complete the linkage.


 Find the nearest Video Teller Machine (VTM) using our [Locator](https://www.dbs.com.sg/index/locator.page?filter=VTM).
