## Pay your loan in full

To pay your loan in full (or redeem your loan) means to pay the outstanding balance of your home loan, thereby closing the loan account.

To redeem your loan, we require either a 2 months' notice or payment of 2 months' interest in lieu of notice for early redemption. [Other fees and charges](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/loans/home-loans/fees-and-charges.page?pid=sg-dbs-pweb-loans-manage-mortgage-txtlink-fees-and-charges) may also apply. While serving the loan redemption notice, your monthly instalment will continue to be deducted from your loan servicing account.
