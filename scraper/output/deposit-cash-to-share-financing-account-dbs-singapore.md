## Deposit Cash to Share Financing Account

You can transfer cash into your Share Financing account via digibank.

  

### To transfer cash to your Share Financing account, input the following payee details:

- Recipient Bank: **DBS Bank**
- Recipient Account Name: **CBG Share Financing**
- Recipient Account Number: **0039574843**
- Provide your **Share Financing Account Number** (e.g. SF 19xxx, SF 319xxx, 320xxx) as recipient reference.

- Follow the steps to transfer cash to your Share Financing account via [digibank](bank-local-funds-transfer-transfer-to-other-dbs-posb-accounts.html).
