## Frequently asked questions

### 

 How can I rack up cashback with my DBS Live Fresh Card?Simply spend at least S$800 on your Card to earn the Bonus Rewards of up to S$120 on your Shopping, Transport and In\-store Overseas Spend for each month.

 What happens if I do not meet the minimum spend requirement of S$800?You will still earn the Base Rewards of 0\.3% cashback on your eligible All Spend.

 What happens if I make a refund?Refunded transaction will be deducted from the Minimum Spend Requirement and excluded in the computation of your cashback.

 When will I receive my cashback?Your cashback will be credited to your Card Account (that is in good standing) by the end of the following calendar month.

Example: The cashback earned for your spending made in the calendar month of March will be credited to your Card Account by end of April.

 How can I find out the merchant category of the posted transactions I made on my credit card account?You may check the merchant category of the posted transactions via DBS digibot:

- Step 1: Click on the **digibot icon** located at the bottom right of this page
- Step 2: Click on **Check Recent Transactions** \> **Check Transactions** and followed by **Proceed to Check Recent Transactions**
- Step 3: Click on **Authenticate me**
- Step 4: Complete the authentication using either your iBanking Log in or Card \& PIN
- Step 5: Select Credit Cards \> Select the Credit Card you wish to enquire on and click on **View Transaction History**
- Step 6: Select the type of transactions (Unbilled Transactions / Billed Transactions) you would like to enquire on
