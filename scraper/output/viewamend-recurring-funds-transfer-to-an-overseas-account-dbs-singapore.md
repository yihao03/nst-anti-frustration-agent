## View/ Amend Recurring Funds Transfer to an Overseas Account

View and amend your scheduled overseas funds transfers instantly via digibank mobile.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to Overseas Funds Transfer](guide-remit.html)

### Important information

- If you have previously applied for a recurring overseas funds transfer via mail\-in form or at our branches, you will not be able to view or amend them via digibank.

### How to view/ amend recurring funds transfer to an overseas account

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*
