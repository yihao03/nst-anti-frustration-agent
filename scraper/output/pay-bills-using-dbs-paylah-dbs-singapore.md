## Pay Bills Using DBS PayLah!

Pay your bills conveniently via DBS PayLah! App.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS PayLah!](guide-paylah.html)

### How to pay bills using DBS PayLah!

- Log in to DBS PayLah! with your **Touch / Face ID** or **PayLah! Password**.
- Tap on **More**.
- Tap on the **AXS** icon and you will be directed to the AXS payment page.
- Select **Pay Bills** and choose your type of bill.
- Enter the relevant information of your bill payment.
- Tap on **Pay with DBS PayLah!**.

### More information

- Your Bill Reference Number is a reference number given by the Billing Organisation and it is usually stated on your bill.
