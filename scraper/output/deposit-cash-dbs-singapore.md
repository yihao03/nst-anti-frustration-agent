## Deposit Cash

Deposit cash into a DBS/POSB Account easily via our Self\-Service Banking Machines available islandwide.

### How to Deposit Cash

[Locate a Cash Deposit Service](https://www.dbs.com.sg/index/locator.page?filter=CAM,BL) near you:   

*Filter âCash Depositâ (for ATM\+) or âBTMâ to check the locations*

- Insert your **ATM/ Debit/ Credit Card**.
- Key in your **6\-Digit Card PIN**.
- Select **Cash Deposit**.
- Select the **Account** that is tagged to your card.
- Place your cash into the cash slot and select **Next**.
- Verify the total deposited amount and select **Confirm**.
- Collect your **ATM/Debit/Credit Card** and **Receipt**.

- Insert your **ATM/ Debit/ Credit Card**.
- Key in your **6\-Digit Card PIN**.
- Select **Cash Deposit**.
- Select **Other DBS/POSB Account**.
- Select the **Account Type**.
- Enter the **Account Number** and select **Confirm**.
- Verify the selected account type and number and select **Next**.
- Place your cash into the cash slot and select **Next**.
- Verify the total deposited amount and select **Confirm**.
- Collect your **ATM/Debit/Credit Card** and **Receipt**.

- Touch the **Screen** and select **Cash Deposit**.
- Select the **Account Type**.
- Enter the **Account Number** and select **Confirm**.
- Verify the selected account type and number and select **Next**.
- Enter your **NRIC/Passport Number** and select **Confirm**.
- Enter your **Mobile Number** and select **Confirm**.
- Place your cash into the cash slot and select **Next**.
- Verify the total deposited amount and select **Confirm**.
- Collect your **Receipt**.

##### Note

- For cardless deposits, **do not** exceed S$20,000 per cash deposit transaction *(subject to notes condition and account limit of $50,000 per day)*.

- Flip your **Passbook** to the last printed page and insert to the machine.
- Select **Cash Deposit**.
- Place your cash into the cash slot and select **Next**.
- Verify the total deposited amount and select **Confirm**.
- Collect your **Passbook** and **Receipt**.

### More information

- **Notes Accepted**:  

	- **ATM\+**: S$10, S$50, S$100, S$1,000 denominations
	- **Branch Teller Machine (BTM and BTM\+)**: S$2, S$5, S$10, S$50, S$100, S$1,000 denominations
- You can deposit a maximum of 200 pieces per cash deposit transaction.
- **Do not** deposit any commemorative notes. *E.g. Bicentennial Notes.*
