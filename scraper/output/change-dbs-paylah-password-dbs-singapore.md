## Change DBS PayLah! Password

Learn how you can update your DBS PayLah! password instantly via DBS PayLah! App.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS PayLah!](guide-paylah.html)

### How to change DBS PayLah! password

- Launch your **DBS PayLah! app** and tap on **Log In**.
- Tap on **Forgot your Password?**.
- Enter your **digibank User ID \& PIN** and tap **Next**.  
*(For non digibank users, enter your **Recovery Email Address**.)*
- Enter the **6\-digit iB Secure PIN** sent to your mobile phone or recovery email address.
- Enter and confirm your **New PayLah! Password** and tap **Next** to complete the reset.

  
  

### More information

- DBS PayLah! Password should be in 6\-16 alphanumeric characters.
