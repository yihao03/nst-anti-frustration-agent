## Check Remit Status

You can check the status of your overseas funds transfer easily via digibank Remit Tracker and DBS digibot.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to Overseas Funds Transfer](guide-remit.html)

  

### Important information

- Overseas funds transfer will generally be completed within 2 to 4 working days.
- Once the funds are transferred to your recipient芒聙聶s bank, it may take a few business days for their bank to credit the amount into their account.

### Track the status of your remittance via Remit Tracker

With Remit Tracker on digibank, you can easily track the status of your remittance to various locations.

- Australia
- Hong Kong
- India
- Japan
- United Arab Emirates
- United Kingdom
- United States
- China *(CNY transfers to DBS China accounts only)*
- Indonesia *(IDR transfers to DBS Indonesia accounts only)*
- Vietnam *(VND transfers to DBS Vietnam accounts only)*

### Learn more about the various DBS Remit Tracker statuses

- **Processing**: Your DBS Remit request is being processed by DBS
- **Sent**: Your DBS Remit has been sent to the recipient芒聙聶s location for our local partner芒聙聶s processing.
- **Completed**: Based on your DBS Remit request, funds have been either credited into your recipient芒聙聶s account or received by your recipient芒聙聶s bank.
- **Unsuccessful**: Funds transfer did not go through successfully and funds will be returned to your bank account. Unsuccessful funds transfer reason is provided by the recipient芒聙聶s bank or our local partner at recipient芒聙聶s location.

*Note: Completed status is only available if the recipient芒聙聶s bank provides an update to DBS.*
