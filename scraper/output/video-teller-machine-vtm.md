### Frequently Asked Questions

 What is the Video Teller Machine (VTM) about?VTM is a teller machine enhanced with live\-video capabilities. You have the option to speak face\-to\-face with a customer service officer if you require assistance from 8\.30am to 6pm daily. Free yourself from regular banking hours and self\-serve key non\-cash banking services 24/7 on VTM.

 What transactions can I perform at the VTM?The following transactions can be self\-service:

- Change of Particulars â Address, Email, Signature
- Balance Enquiry
- Debit Card Issuance / Replacement
- Reset Card PIN
- Amend Card Settings â Card Withdrawal Limits, Account Linkage
- ATM Card Issuance / Replacement
- Instant Passbook Replacement

The following transactions require face\-to\-face Video Teller Assistance which is only available from 8\.30am to 6pm daily

- Change of Particulars â Mobile
- Change GIRO Payment Limit
- Delete GIRO Arrangement
- Request Additional Cheque Book (via Mail Out)
- Replacement of Passbook (Via Mail Out)

The following transactions are only available at branches\* during branch operating hours:

- Change of Particulars \- Passport

\*Only branches with counter services or financial advisory services

 What verification documents do I need to use the VTM?You will need your ATM/Debit card for verification. Or you can use your NRIC (Singaporeans and Malaysians) or passport if you do not have your ATM/Debit card with you.   
  
Bring along your mobile phone with a registered number with the bank, for faster authentication experience.

 I am unable to proceed with self\-service and require assistanceOur on\-screen customer service officers will be there to assist you from 8\.30am to 6pm daily.

 Can other people hear me when I am speaking to a customer service officer?No, you can conduct your banking within the enclosed VTM cubicle for privacy.

 What languages are offered on the VTM?English, Mandarin, Malay and Tamil language are currently offered at our machines.
