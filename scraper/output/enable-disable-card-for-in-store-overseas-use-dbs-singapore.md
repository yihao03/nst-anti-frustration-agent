## Enable/ Disable Card For In Store Overseas Use

Learn how to enable / disable your card's overseas usage easily using digibank or at our DBS/POSB ATM.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Go Travel with DBS](guide-travel.html), [Your Guide to Card Security](guide-card-security.html)

### Important information

- For your security, magnetic stripe transactions for overseas point\-of\-sale and ATM access on all DBS/POSB cards are disabled by default to reduce the risk of unauthorized transactions. For eCommerce, EMV chip, and contactless transactions will remain enabled for overseas use.
- When travelling overseas, enable **Overseas contactless and card chip** control to allow in\-store purchases. Overseas contactless and card chip payments use EMV chip technology, offering a more secure option through cryptographic processes.
- **Overseas magnetic stripe** is located within your Payment Controls dashboard. Turn it on only if absolutely required. We strongly recommend using contactless or chip methods. Magnetic stripe transactions are less secure and more vulnerable to fraud, like skimming and cloning. You'll still find magnetic stripe payments in some countries, such as the USA, South Korea, and Japan.
- You are advised to disable your overseas controls if you are in Singapore.

### How to enable/ disable Card for in store overseas use

There are various channels which you may enable/ disable card for in store overseas use with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.
