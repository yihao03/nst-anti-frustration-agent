## Amend or Cancel your Overseas Funds Transfer

Visit any DBS/POSB Branch with your NRIC/Passport to apply for an amendment or cancellation of your wrong transfer.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to Overseas Funds Transfer](guide-remit.html)

### Important information

- Currency and amount cannot be amended. If you need to correct these, you will need to cancel the original payment and submit a new payment request.
- Cancellation is only possible if the funds have not yet reached the recipient's account.
- Any refund made will be subjected to the prevailing exchange rates.

### Fees \& Charges


 Amendments and cancellations are processed on a best\-effort basis and are subject to the following charges:
 - **Amendments:** S$30 per transaction, plus any agent bank charges
- **Cancellations:** S$35 per transaction, plus any agent bank charges


 You may also incur additional foreign exchange losses.
