## Repay Home Loan using CPF Funds

Learn how you can use CPF Funds for your home loan.

### How to repay home loan using CPF funds

##### Simply follow these steps:

- Visit [CPF Website](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://www.cpf.gov.sg/Members&3rdparty=%27CPF%27) and login with your **Singpass**.
- Click on **Tools and services** at the top of the page and select **Forms and e\-applications**.
- Scroll down to **Home ownership**, click onto **Manage CPF usage for your home** and select **Apply online**.
- Select your property under **Property Details** and click **Next**.
- Select **Commence Monthly Instalment** and click **Next**.
- Click **Start** and complete the following:
	- Financier: **DBS Bank Ltd**
	- Monthly Instalment: Indicate your **monthly instalment amount**
	- Effective From: Select **the month to commence the monthly instalment**
- Read the **Terms \& Conditions** and select the checkbox to provide your declaration, then click **Next**.
- Review and confirm that the information you have provided is correct then click **Submit**.
- Please note that you may also **Revise Monthly Instalment, Cease Monthly Instalment** or **Make Arrears Payment** following the steps above.

##### Simply follow these steps:

- Visit [CPF Website](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://www.cpf.gov.sg/Members&3rdparty=%27CPF%27) and login with your **Singpass**.
- Click on **Tools and services** at the top of the page and select **Forms and e\-applications**.
- Scroll down to **Home ownership**, click onto **Manage CPF usage for your home** and select **Apply online**.
- Select your property under **Property Details** and click **Next**.
- Select **Make Lump Sum Payment** and click **Next**.
- Click **Start** and complete the following:
	- Financier: **DBS Bank Ltd**
	- Amount: Key in your **CPF repayment amount**
	- Deduction Date: Indicate the deduction date as **3 working days** before the payment date of your full/ partial repayment request
- Read the **Terms \& Conditions** and select the checkbox to provide your declaration, then click **Next**.
- Review and confirm that the information you have provided is correct then click **Submit**.
