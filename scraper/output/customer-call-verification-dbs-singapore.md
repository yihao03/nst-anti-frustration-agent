## Customer Call Verification

With impersonation scams on the rise, it can be tricky to tell if a call is real or a scam. With Customer Call Verification, we have an added layer of security that helps you verify the authenticity of any call claiming to be from DBS/POSB with your digibank app.

### Important information

- You must have digital token enabled on your digibank mobile app to approve this call verification.
- Ensure you have push notifications enabled for digibank mobile app to receive the call verification request.
