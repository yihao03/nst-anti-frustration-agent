## Check if a Payment Facility is set up for your DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

Check Your DBS Vickers Online Payment Facility.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### Important information

- If your DBS Multi\-Currency Account is linked to your DBS Vickers trading account, you do not need to maintain any minimum balance for the linked DBS Multi\-Currency Account as the fall\-below\-fee will be waived.
- If your EPS account is not with DBS you will need to check the steps with your respective banks.

### How to check if a Payment Facility is set up for your DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

There are various channels which you may check if a Payment Facility is set up for your DBS Vickers Online Trading Account with us. The most convenient method would be via DBS Vicker Securities.

**For Payment of Purchased shares:**  
  

- **SGD**: Make a bill payment to DBS Vickers Securities, indicate your DBS Vickers online account number in the bill reference number.
- **Foreign Currency**: Remittance to DBS Vickers Securities, receipt of remittance emailed to [\[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection) and indicate the DBS Vickers online account number.
