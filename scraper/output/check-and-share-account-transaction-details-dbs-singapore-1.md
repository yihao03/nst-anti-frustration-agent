## Check and Share Account Transaction Details

You can check your transactions using digibank, DBS digibot and Phone Banking.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to digibank online](guide-ibanking.html)

### Important information

- You can [view your eStatements and eAdvices](bank-statements-viewing-estatements.html) for transactions that are more than 6 months ago.
- For PayLah! transactions, log in via DBS PayLah! app. Learn more on how to [check PayLah! transaction history](bank-ssb-paylah-transaction-history.html).

### How to check and share your account transaction details

There are various channels which you may check your account transactions with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

##### **Click on the steps to view screens**

##### Launch our DBS digibot now!

[Get Started](https://chatbanking.dbs.com/mbsg/GCE/G200HS002)

- Dial **1800 111 1111 (from Singapore)** or **(65\) 6327 2265 (from Overseas)** for DBS Personal Banking Hotline (24/7\).
- For a menu in English **Press 1**, for Mandarin **Press 2**.
- Enter your **NRIC, Debit or Credit Card Number**  **followed by \#** to proceed.
- Follow the steps for **SMS OTP** to proceed.
- Select **Deposit Account Related Services**.
- Press **1** for Account Balance(s).
- Press **2** for Last 5 Transactions.
	- Enter your **Account Number** OR;
	- **Press \#** to list all the Accounts.

This includes but is not limited to any card transaction in Singapore dollars on overseas\-based websites and mobile applications. Learn more about [overseas card transaction fees](card-charges-and-fees-overseas-transaction-fees.html?pid=sg-dbs-help-support-bank-deposit-accounts-account-transactions.html).

- Call DBS Fraud Reporting hotline 1800 339 6963 or (65\) 6339 6963 from overseas.
- DBS will investigate, and we will credit the disputed amount within 7 working days for successful cases. Should further verification be required, we will reach out to you within 7 working days.
