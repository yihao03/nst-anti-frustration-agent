## Deposit Account Minimum Balance Service Charge

Your deposit account will incur this service charge when your balance falls below the minimum average daily balance.

### Important information

- To avoid incurring the Service Charge, ensure that your account meets the Minimum Average Daily Balance.
- Your Average Daily Balance is the total amount of your account balances at the end of each day, divided by the number of days in the month.

	- **First 15 days:** Your account had **$100 every day**  
	→ $100 Ã 15 days \= ***$1,500***
	- **Next 15 days:** Your account had **$400 every day**  
	→ $400 Ã 15 days \= ***$6,000***- **Total of daily balances:** $1,500 \+ $6,000 \= ***$7,500***- **Average Daily Balance:** $7,500 Ã· 30 days \= ***$250***
