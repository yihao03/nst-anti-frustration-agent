TravellerShield Plus is underwritten by Chubb Insurance Singapore Limited and distributed by DBS Bank Ltd.

### How to submit TravellerShield Plus claim


 Simply:
 - Ensure you have all the required documents ready for submission
- Then, [submit your claim online](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://ap.studio.chubb.com/myaccount/sg/default/default/default/en-SG&3rdparty=%27chubb%27).

  

**Important Note:**
- Your claim must be submitted within 30 days of the event giving rise to your claim.
- If you need to seek medical treatment in Singapore after returning from your trip, you must do so within 72 hours after your return to Singapore.
