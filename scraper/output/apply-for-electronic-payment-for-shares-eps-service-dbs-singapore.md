## Apply for Electronic Payment for Shares (EPS) Service


 Electronic Payment of Shares (EPS) is a hassle\-free way to settle your shares transaction.  
  

 
 Conveniently pay for Singapore shares at ATMs or via digibank online and have sale proceeds automatically credited into your designated bank account. You also enjoy flexibility in paying by contract or in lump sum.  
  

 
 Simply apply for Electronic Payment for Shares (EPS) Service via the [Payment Facility form](https://www.dbsvickers.com/iwov-resources/media/vickers-pdf/Payment_Facility_June_2022.pdf).

### How to apply for Electronic Payment for Shares (EPS) service

Complete the [Payment Facility form](https://www.dbsvickers.com/iwov-resources/media/vickers-pdf/Payment_Facility_June_2022.pdf) and mail in to us via the enclosed Business Reply Envelope

- Application for payment facility takes approximately 2\-3 weeks to process upon receipt of the completed application form.
