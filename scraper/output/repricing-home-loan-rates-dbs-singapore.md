Repricing Home Loan Rates## Reprice my Home Loan

Home Loan repayment is a big expense item and could form a large proportion of your monthly expenses.

If you are now eligible to switch from your existing DBS Home Loan to a new loan package, choose from our various Fixed or Floating rate packages, depending on your needs.

## Residential Property

Explore repricing your existing DBS Home Loan for interest savings or switch to a loan package that best suits your current need.

Our repricing packages are currently pegged to the Fixed Deposit Home Rate 24 (FHR24\). FHR24 refers to the prevailing 24 months Singapore Dollar fixed deposit interest rate of DBS Bank for amounts S$1,000 to S$9,999 or such other sum as we may specify. FHR24 is currently 0\.900% p.a.

## Eligibility Criteria

- Existing DBS Home Loan which is out of the lock\-in period
- Minimum outstanding loan amount of S$100,000
- Minimum outstanding loan period of 5 Years

We will inform you the repricing fees and prepayment charges (for loans within lock\-in period) where applicable.
