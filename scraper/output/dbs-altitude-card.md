## Fancy getting more miles out of your go\-to travel card?

You can receive **10,000 bonus miles** when you pay for your annual fee.

- 10,000 bonus miles (5,000 DBS Points) will be credited to your account when the annual fee of S$196\.20 (inclusive of GST) is charged to your card.
- If the annual fee is subsequently waived, the 5,000 DBS Points credited will be reversed within 3 working days upon annual fee waiver.

  
Miles are awarded in the form of DBS Points for every S$5 spend (1 DBS Point \= 2 miles) on a per transaction basis. Frequent flyer programmes include KrisFlyer and AsiaMiles.
