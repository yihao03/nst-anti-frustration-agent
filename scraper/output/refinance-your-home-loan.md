 What are 3M SORA and FHR?

| Definitions of pricing terms | |
| --- | --- |
| 3M SORA | The Singapore Overnight Rate Average (SORA) is an interest rate benchmark, and it reflects the actual transactions between banks, made between 8am and 6\.15pm in Singapore.3M SORA refers to 3M compounded SORA rate which is based on a compounding period of 3 months of the historical SORA rate.SORA fluctuates on a daily basis, so if you re\-taking a housing loan with your interest rate pegged to SORA, your loan repayments will change too.[Find out more on the prevailing 3M SORA Rate](https://www.dbs.com.sg/iwov-resources/pdf/loan/home-loans/manage-your-mortgage/sora_dbs.pdf). |
| FHR | FHR6 refers to Fixed Deposit Home Rate 6, a reference rate unique to DBS. FHR6 is calculated based on the prevailing 6 months Singapore dollar fixed deposit interest rate of DBS Bank for amounts within S$1,000 to S$9,999\.[Find out more on the prevailing FHR](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/rates-online/singapore-dollar-fixed-deposits.page?pid=sg-dbs-pweb-loans-refinance-private-property-loan-txtlink-rates-sdfd). |

 When should I refinance my home loan?Generally, it may be a good time to consider refinancing when your lock in and cash rebate claw back periods for your current housing loan has ended and your loan interest rates is significantly higher then the interest rates being offered to you.

 Is there any cost to refinancing my home loan to DBS from another bank?There will be legal and valuation costs. However, we do offer cash rebates of up to $2,800, depending on your loan amount, to help subsidize these costs.
