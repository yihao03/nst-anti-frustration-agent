## Terminate GIRO Arrangement

Learn how you can terminate a GIRO arrangement easily using digibank.

### How to terminate GIRO arrangement

There are various channels which you may terminate your GIRO arrangement with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen )
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

### More information

- You can specify an instruction to terminate your linkages on the next day or any day within a month ahead.
- If you are residing overseas, without access to digibank online, complete and mail in our [Amendment/Termination of GIRO Form](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/iwov-resources/docs/giro-01_0319dbs.pdf).
