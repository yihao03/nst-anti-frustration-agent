## Reset/Retrieve digibank User ID and/or PIN

You can reset or retrieve your User ID and reset your PIN using our digital channels. 

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to digibank](guide-ibanking.html)

### Important information

- digibank User ID must be between 5 to 20 characters and contains letters and numbers only.
- To retrieve your digibank User ID online, you need to have a registered iB Secure Device or registered mobile to receive SMS One\-Time\-Password (OTP).
- digibank PIN must be between 6 to 9 digit numbers.

### How to reset digibank PIN

There are various channels which you may reset your digibank PIN with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

- Launch your digibank mobile app and select **Log In**.
- Tap **Forgot your PIN?**, and indicate your **Identity Type**, **Identity Number** and **Date of Birth**.
- Tap **Next** and select the option that you like to identify yourself via:
	- Debit / ATM Card; OR
	- DBS Secure Device (Token)
- After identification, **Enter New PIN** and tap on **Set New PIN** to complete the reset of your digibank PIN.
