## Overseas Withdrawal Charges

Withdrawal fees are waived if you withdraw using Plus/Cirrus Card via Westpac Group ATMs in Australia and DBS Hong Kong, and Plus Card via DBS ATMs in India and Indonesia. There will be a fee for withdrawals via all other overseas ATMs.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Go Travel with DBS](guide-travel.html)

### Important information

- For DBS Visa Debit card that is linked to a Multi\-Currency Account, overseas cash withdrawals are directly debited from the respective foreign currency wallet, provided there are sufficient funds in that currency.
- For each cash withdrawal, a service charge of S$7 will be billed in the equivalent foreign currency (e.g., US$5 will be charged for your US Dollar cash withdrawal in New York). [View list of fees in foreign currency](https://www.dbs.com.sg/iwov-resources/pdf/cards/mca_atm_fees.pdf).

| Card Brand/ Association | ATM | Fees |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **MasterCard** | - Westpac Group ATMs in Australia(Westpac, St. George Bank, Bank of Melbourne and BankSA) - DBS Hong Kong | Free |
| - ATMs of Bank of Central Asia (BCA, Indonesia) - ATMs of HDFC (India) - Bank of Philippines Island (Philippines) | S$2\.00 |
| - All other overseas ATMs | S$7\.00 |
| **VISA Card** | - Westpac Group ATMs in Australia(Westpac, St. George Bank, Bank of Melbourne and BankSA) - DBS Hong Kong - DBS India and DBS Indonesia ATMs | Free |
| - ATMs of HDFC (India) | S$2\.00 |
| - All other overseas ATMs | S$7\.00 |
| **UnionPay Card** | - All overseas ATMs | Waived |
