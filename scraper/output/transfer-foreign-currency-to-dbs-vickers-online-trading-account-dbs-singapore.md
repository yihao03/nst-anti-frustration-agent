## Transfer Foreign Currency to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

Transfer foreign currencies into your DBS Vickers Online Trading Account. *(For accounts applied before 15 January 2018\)*

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

- After making deposits to the above accounts, send us a **screenshot** of the completed transaction, indicate your **name** and DBS Vickers **account number** and email us at [\[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#7811161e17550b1f381c1a0b0e17161411161d561b1715).

- Visit any DBS Branch by using our [Locator](https://www.dbs.com.sg/index/locator.page).
- Perform the foreign currency transfer from your **DBS Multi\-Currency Account (MCA)** to your **DBS Vickers Online Trading Account** at the branch.
- Write your **Name** and **DBS Vickers Online Trading Account number** on the pay\-in slip.
- Email to [\[email protected]](/cdn-cgi/l/email-protection#94fdfaf2fbb9e7f3d4f0f6e7e2fbfaf8fdfaf1baf7fbf9).

**Note**
- Currencies Accepted: **AUD, CAD, EUR, JPY, HKD, GBP** and **USD**.
- This is a free service.
- Transfers take up to 2 working days.
