## Troubleshooting Guide

Have trouble with your digibank or Paylah! app? Follow these troubleshooting steps.

### What issue are you facing?

##### Step 1


 Check if App is updated to the latest version.
 - **digibank mobile**  

 Download the latest digibank mobile app
- **DBS Paylah**  
Click the link below to download the latest app version:

##### Step 2


 Check if Mobile Phone Operating Systems is the latest version.  

 You can choose to enable auto OS update or follow these steps:  

- iOS devices  

 Settings \> General \> Software Update
- Android devices:  

 Settings \> Software Update

##### Step 3


 Restart your phone.  

 If you are facing the same issue after restarting, you may delete your app and reinstall the latest app version. 
 

##### Step 1


 Check our [Scheduled Maintenance](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/deposits/maintenance-schedule.page).  
  

##### Step 2

 
 Attempt your login again.
 - Clear your cache after each failed login before you attempt your login again.
- If your login is still unsuccessful after repeated tries, it could be your Internet Service Provider (ISP), Local Area Network (LAN), or our system is facing intermittent slowness/ problems. In this case, you should attempt your log in a while later.

##### Step 3


 Incorrect login credentials
 - If you have forgotten your digibank login, you may reset your digibank [User ID](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-digibank-reset-uid-pin.html) or [PIN](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-digibank-reset-uid-pin.html) accordingly.
- If you have forgotten your PayLah! login, click on ["Forgot your Password?"](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-ssb-reset-paylah.html)


 You can reset your [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin) to reactivate your account. Learn more on how to [Reset digibank PIN](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-digibank-reset-uid-pin.html).
 


 Click on the links below to learn how to resolve common issues faced related to digital token:
 - [Unable to setup digital token](bank-ibanking-digital-token-setup.html)
- [Poor/No internet connection](bank-ibanking-digital-token-no-internet.html)
- [Non Receipt of Email OTP](bank-ibanking-digital-token-additional-security.html#email-otp)
- [Set up your Digital Token on multiple devices](bank-ibanking-digital-token-multiple-devices.html)


 For any other issues faced, refer to [Troubleshooting your Digital Token](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-ibanking-digital-token-troubleshoot.html).
 


 If you are able to login to your digibank mobile app to change your User ID/PIN you may follow these steps:  

- Log in to digibank mobile with your **Touch / Face ID** or **digibank User ID \& PIN**.
- Tap on **More** at the bottom and under **Your profile**, select **Change User ID/PIN**.
- Enter your existing and new **PIN** and tap **Change PIN now**.


 If you no longer have access to your digibank Online or digibank mobile app, please call 1800 111 1111 for assistance to close your profile so that you may reapply. 
 

### More information

- Updating your OS can help minimise service disruption and protect your device against malicious attacks. Learn more about the [minimum operating system (OS) requirements for DBS apps](bank-minimum-operating-system-requirements.html).
