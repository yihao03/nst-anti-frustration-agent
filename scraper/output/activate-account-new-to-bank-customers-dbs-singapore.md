## Activate Account (New to Bank Customers)

Activate your Savings Account immediately via digibank or a [DBS/POSB ATM](card-activate-new-card.html#dbsposb-atm). To activate your Current Account simply visit any DBS/POSB Branch with your NRIC/Passport.

### How to activate account (New to Bank Customers)

- Upon successful application of your **New Savings Account**.
- You will receive a **Debit Card** and **PIN** within 5 working days.
- Follow our guide to activate your new debit card via [digibank mobile](card-activate-new-card.html#digibank mobile), digibank [digibank online](card-activate-new-card.html#digibank-online) or via a [DBS/POSB ATM](card-activate-new-card.html#dbsposb-atm).
- Your Savings Account will be activated concurrently with your Debit Card activation.

- Upon successful application of your DBS eMulti\-Currency Autosave Plus.
- Visit any [DBS/POSB Branch](https://www.dbs.com.sg/index/locator.page) with your **NRIC/Passport** to activate your account and the chequebook will be mailed to you registered mailing address with the bank.
