## Add Cheque or Demand Draft Recipient

Add a cheque or demand draft recipient easily using digibank online.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to digibank](guide-ibanking.html)

Consider using [Electronic Deferred Payment (EDP)](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/deposits/pay-with-ease/electronic-deferred-payment?pid=sg-dbs-help-support-bank-cheque-purchase-ibcheque-add-recipient)1, a new, free service providing a streamlined digital alternative to cheques and cashier's orders.  
 Benefits of EDP: 

- Search for recipients via PayNow or account number
- Send or receive an EDP via digibank app instantly
- Easy and convenient tracking

*1Ensure your recipient has a valid account with EDP participating banks (Citibank, DBS Bank, HSBC, Maybank, OCBC Singapore, Standard Chartered Bank and UOB)*
