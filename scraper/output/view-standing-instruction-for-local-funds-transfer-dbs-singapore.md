## View Standing Instruction for Local Funds Transfer

Find out how to view your existing recurring local funds transfer via digibank online.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to digibank](guide-ibanking.html)

### Important information

- Viewing of your existing standing instructions is only available via digibank online and it is currently not available on digibank mobile. Please follow the guide below to view your existing standing instructions.

### How to view Standing Instruction for local funds transfer

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

### More information

- Only Standing Instructions for local funds transfer to another account will be shown.
- Standing Instructions using Cashier's Order, Demand Draft and Telegraphic Transfer (Overseas Funds Transfer) will not be shown on this page.
