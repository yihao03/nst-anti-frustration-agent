### How to top up mobile prepaid number

There are various channels which you may top up mobile prepaid number with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

- Log in to digibank mobile with your **Touch / Face ID** or **digibank User ID \& PIN**.
- Tap on **More** at the bottom, under **Other Services** select **Top Up Mobile Prepaid**.
- Select the **Telco** (Service Provider), enter your mobile number and tap **Next**.
- Select your preferred **Account** to pay from, the **Top up** options and tap **Next**.
- Verify your details and tap **Top up now** to complete the top up request.
