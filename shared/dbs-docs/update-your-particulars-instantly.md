## How it works

Hereâs a simple guide on how to update your address.

### On your digibank mobile app

|  | Step 1 : Log in and tap on **More**.Step 2 : Tap **Update Particulars** and select **Address Details** at top barStep 3 : Tap **NEXT** and tap **UPDATE**Step 4 : Enter the new postal code, tap **FIND ADDRESS**. Input Unit No. (if any), then tap **SAVE**Step 5 : Select **Same as the residential address** if you would like to change your mailing address to be the same as your residential addressStep 6 : Tap the account(s) which will use the new address, then tap **NEXT**Step 7 : Review your changes and tap **CONFIRM** to update your Address Details |
