## Manage Your Car Loan

Are you unsure of how to manage your car loan? Let us help you out! Learn how to retrieve important details about your car loan, including your outstanding payment amount, and discover the best methods for making payments.

**Note**: Effective December 10 2024, the Standard Terms and Conditions governing for Hire Purchase Agreements will be updated. Refer to the latest [terms and conditions](https://www.dbs.com.sg/iwov-resources/pdf/loan/auto-loan/hire_purchase.pdf).

### How to check your car loan information

You may check your car loan details listed below using our DBS digibot:

- Payment Method Arrangement (Debit from DBS/POSB or Other Bank)
- Giro Start Date
- Payment Due Date

### How to check and pay your full settlement amount

The service listed below can be found using our DBS digibot: 

- Check full settlement amount
- Request a copy to show your car dealer
- Pay full settlement amount from your DBS/POSB account

### How to repay your car loan

There are several ways to repay your monthly instalment. The most convenient method is linking your loan account with DBS/POSB account. For one\-time payment, submit your request via DBS digibot.
