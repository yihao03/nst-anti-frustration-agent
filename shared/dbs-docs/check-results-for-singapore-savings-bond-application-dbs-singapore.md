## Check Results for Singapore Savings Bond Application

Application results will be available [online](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://eservices.mas.gov.sg/statistics/fdanet/AllotmentResult.aspx&3rdparty=%27Monetary Authority of Singapore%27) after 3pm on the third last business day of the month.

  

- MAS will announce the overall application results on the third last business day of the month, and results can be viewed [online](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://eservices.mas.gov.sg/statistics/fdanet/AllotmentResult.aspx&3rdparty=%27Monetary Authority of Singapore%27).
- If your application is successful, you will be notified by CDP via mail of the amount of Savings Bonds allotted to you. You can also check your holdings online through the [CDP Internet service](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://www1.cdp.sgx.com/sgx-cdp-web/login&3rdparty=%27SGX%27) or by calling CDP at 6535 7511\.
- For all unsuccessful/invalid applications, monies will be refunded by the second last business day of the month.
