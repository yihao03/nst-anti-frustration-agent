## Cheque deposit services

Find a [nearest Quick Cheque Deposit](https://www.dbs.com.sg/index/locator.page?filter=CHQ) location, or mail your cheques to us at:

 **DBS Bank  
Robinson Road Post Office  
PO Box 1235  
Singapore 902435**

### 

 Before you drop your cheque off1. Check that your deposit instructions are written at the back of the cheque.

	- Full name as per your bank account records
	- Bank account number
	- Contact numberWe recommend crossing your cheque and cross out "or Bearer".
2. Take note of the cut\-off timings for quick cheque deposits. Timings vary according to the type of currency.
3. Mailed\-in cheques are subjected to local mail delivery times and processed in **2 working days** upon receiving them.

We recommend taking a photo of your cheque before posting it.
