## Documents Required for Home Loan Repricing

Find out the process in obtaining the necessary documents and submit your Home Loan Repricing application.

If your property is an Investment Property or Owner Occupied Property with Cash Out (Term Loan), there are 2 ways for us to assess your repricing application: Total Debt Servicing Ratio (TDSR) assessment or Debt Reduction Plan (DRP).

- Total Debt Servicing Ratio (TDSR)  

 Kindly ensure that you have consolidated all the documents per listed for us to assess the TDSR and process your request. A copy of each document is required from **all borrowers** unless otherwise specified.
  
- Debt Reduction Plan (DRP)  

 You may reprice your loan without going through TDSR assessment if you repay at least 3% of your total outstanding loan amount upfront or over a period of not more than 3 years under the DRP.
