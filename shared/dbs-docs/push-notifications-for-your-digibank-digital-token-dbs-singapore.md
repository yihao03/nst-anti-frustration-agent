## Push notifications for your digibank/ Digital Token

Enable push notifications to enjoy a secured \& seamless banking experience at your fingertips.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Guide to your Digital Token](bank-ibanking-digital-token.html)

### Important information

- To receive push notifications, ensure that you have enabled push notifications and allowed permissions for digibank app.

### How to enable push notifications for your digibank/ Digital Token

- Go to your phone **Settings**, select **Notifications**.
- Select **DBS digibank SG**.
- Under **Allow Notifications**, toggle the **Switch On**.

- Go to your phone **Settings**, select **Notifications**.
- Tap on **App notifications** and select **DBS digibank SG**.
- Under **Allow Notifications**, toggle the **Switch On**.

### More information

- Push notifications may not be received if you are in a low network coverage area.
- If you do not receive the push notification for your Digital Token, learn more on how to [Authenticate transactions with your Digital Token](bank-ibanking-digital-token-transaction.html#manual-digital-token).
