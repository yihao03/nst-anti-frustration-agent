## Processing Time for Share Transfers

Find out how long it takes for Share Transfers to be processed.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### Important information

- Scripless transfer is a securities trade established without the issuance or exchange of a physical certificate.
- Please ensure correct instructions are given to both DBS Vickers and the other Share Counterparty / Custodian in order to effect a smooth shares transfer transaction.

### What is the processing time for share transfers

| Submitted Scripless Transfer In Form | Share status can be checked from [DBS Vickers Online](https://www1.dbsvonline.com/OTS/English/Login) |
| --- | --- |
| HK Stocks | 3 working days after the other brokerage has completed the transfer out |
| US Stocks | 4 working days after the other brokerage has completed the transfer out |
| CAD Stocks | 6 working days after the other brokerage has completed the transfer out |
| UK Stocks | 6 working days after the other brokerage has completed the transfer out |

| Submitted Scripless Transfer Out Form | Processing Time for transfer out request |
| --- | --- |
| HK Stocks | 3 working days upon receipt of instruction |
| US Stocks | 3 working days upon receipt of instruction |
| CAD Stocks | 3 working days upon receipt of instruction |
| UK Stocks | 3 working days upon receipt of instruction |
