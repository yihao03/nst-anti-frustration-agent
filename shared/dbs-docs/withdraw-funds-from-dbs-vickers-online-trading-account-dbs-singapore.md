## Withdraw Funds from DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

Perform a withdrawal online easily and receive funds via GIRO or EPS (Electronic Payment for Shares).

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### Important information

- If you have a DBS Multi\-Currency Account (MCA) [Payment Facility set up](investment-vickers-check-payment-facility.html), you can transfer funds directly in and out of your MCA online through digibank.

### How to withdraw funds from DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

- [Log in](https://www1.dbsvonline.com/OTS/English/Login) to DBS Vickers Online.
- Under **My Account**, select **Account Management**.
- Under **Online Instructions**, select **Online Funds Withdrawal**.
- Select a **Withdrawal Reason** (Pure Withdrawal or Intra Account Transfer \- SGD only).
- Select a **Mode of Payment**
	- GIRO (Existing facility needed)
	- EPS (Existing facility needed)
- Enter your **Withdrawal Amount** and select a **Currency to be paid in**.
- Click **Submit** to confirm your withdrawal request.

### More information

- Cut\-off time is 9:00am daily for same day fund withdrawals. For withdrawal requests submitted after 9:00am, crediting to your account will be done on the next business day.
- Withdrawals cannot be deposited to a SGD or Foreign Currency Fixed Deposit Account.
