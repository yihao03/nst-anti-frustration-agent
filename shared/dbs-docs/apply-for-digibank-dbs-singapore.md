## Apply for digibank

Sign up for digibank to enjoy convenient banking anytime, anywhere. With digibank, you will gain access to a full range of digital services.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to digibank](guide-ibanking.html)

### Important information

- For **new to DBS or POSB customers**, it is highly recommended to [Register for Singpass](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://www.singpass.gov.sg/home/ui/register/instructions&3rdparty=%27Singpass%27) so that you can open an account instantly.
- For **digibank registration (mobile or online)**, if you have an ATM, Debit or Credit Card, ensure your card is activated before applying for digibank. Learn more on how you can [Activate New Card](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/card-activate-new-card.html?pid=sg-dbs-help-and-support-bank-ibanking-application).â
- **Always keep your contact details** updated and set transaction alerts to be informed instantly of any transactions on your account(s).
- **DO NOT** disclose **your User ID, PIN or other identification numbers** to anyone and **NEVER** save your password on your device.â
- Learn how to [\#BSHARP](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/deposits/bank-with-ease/protecting-yourself-online) and bank safely online.

### How to Apply for digibank

There are various channels which you may Apply for digibank with us.

Customers who are at least 16 years old and does not have an existing account with DBS or POSB can open their first account directly using DBS digibank mobile.â

 Your application will be processed within 3 working days after we receive your complete application. Once approved, you will receive your debit card within 3\-5 working days then proceed with the following steps:
 1. Activate your debit card. Learn more on how to [Activate New Card](card-activate-new-card.html?pid=sg-dbs-help-and-support-bank-ibanking-application-non-singpass-txtlink-debit-card-activation).
2. Once your card is activated, proceed to [digibank mobile Registration](bank-ibanking-application.html?pid=sg-dbs-help-and-support-bank-ibanking-application-non-singpass-txtlink-digibank-mobile-registration#digibank-mobile).
3. Once registered, login in digibank and [set up your Digital Token](bank-ibanking-digital-token-setup.html?pid=sg-dbs-help-and-support-bank-ibanking-application-non-singpass-txtlink-set-up-your-digital-token) when prompted.
