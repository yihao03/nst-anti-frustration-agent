GIRO Billing OrganisationThese are the list of participating Billing Organisations (BOs) which you can apply for GIRO via digibank online. Please refer to the table below for the bill reference number of each GIRO BO.  
  
GIRO application using Card and Pin can be made for BOs denoted with an \*.

For BOs not listed here, please obtain GIRO form from your BO, fill it up and submit directly to the billing Organisation. If you have not provided your signature, your paper GIRO application will be rejected. We recommend you to fill in the online GIRO application instead. If online GIRO application is not available, please provide your signature at our DBS/POSB branch.

Click [here](http://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/deeplink/000000002110026)脗聽to set up a GIRO arrangement. For more information on GIRO, you may wish to check out our [Guide to GIRO Arrangements](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-payment-setup-giro-arrangement.html).
