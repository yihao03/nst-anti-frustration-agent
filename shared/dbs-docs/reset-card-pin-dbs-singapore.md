## Reset Card PIN

Change your Card PIN immediately via digibank or Video Teller Machine (VTM).

*Part of: Guides \>* [Go Travel with DBS](guide-travel.html)

### Important information

- Your PIN for Corporate ATM/Debit/Credit Cards will arrive in a separate mailer from your card.

### How to reset card PIN

There are various channels which you may reset card PIN with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*


 Locate [our nearest Video Teller Machine (VTM)](https://www.dbs.com.sg/index/locator.page?filter=VTM).  
  

**Authenticate by Card**
- Insert your ATM/Debit/Credit Card and attempt to complete the authentication process.
- After multiple failed attempts, you will be prompted to reset your PIN with another identification method.

**Authenticate by NRIC/Passport**
- Scan your NRIC or Passport to identify yourself.
- An Officer will be connected to assist you to reset your PIN.

**Note:** PIN reset requests for customers identified with NRIC or Passport is available between 9am to 6pm daily.
