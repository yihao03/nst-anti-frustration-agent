## Check Credit Card Available Limit

You can simply check your Credit Card Available limit via digibank and Phone Banking.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Go Travel with DBS](guide-travel.html), [Go Shopping with DBS](guide-shopping.html)

### How to check credit card available limit

There are various channels which you may check your credit card available limit with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

- Dial **1800 111 1111 (from Singapore)** or **(\+65\) 6327 2265 (from Overseas)**.
- For a menu in English **Press 1**, for Mandarin **Press 2**.
- Enter your **NRIC, Debit or Credit Card Number followed by \#** to proceed.
- Follow the steps for **SMS OTP** to proceed.
- Select **Credit Card Services**.
- Press **2** for Outstanding and **Available Balance**.
