## Redeem Singapore Savings Bonds

Redeem your Savings Bonds easily via digibank or our DBS/POSB ATMs.

### Important information

- The redemption period opens at **6\.00pm** on the **1st business day of the month** and closes at **9\.00pm** on the **4th last business day** of the month.
- Redemption is only available on **Monday to Saturday; 7:00am to 9:00pm (excluding public holidays)**.
- A non\-refundable transaction fee of **S$2** will be charged for each redemption request.
- **Do not submit redemption requests for bonds maturing the following month** *(e.g., submitting a redemption request in September for a bond maturing in October)* **as the funds will be automatically credited to your account upon maturity.**
- Submitted redemption requests cannot be cancelled or amended.

### How to redeem Singapore Savings Bonds (SSB)

There are various channels which you may redeem Singapore Savings Bonds with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

- Log in to digibank mobile with your **Touch / Face ID** or **digibank User ID \& PIN**.
- Tap on **digiWealth** at the bottom navigation.
- Under **Invest**, tap on **Show all** and select **SGS**.
- Tap **Redeem** and select **Bought with cash**.
- Enter the **Issue Code** for the SSB you would like to redeem and tap **Next**.
- Enter your **Redemption amount** *(in multiples of S$500 with upper limit of S$200,000\)*.
- Verify the **CDP account number** populated.
- Select your preferred **Account** for service fee deduction and tap **Next**.
 - Verify your Redemption Details and tap **Submit** to confirm your redemption.

##### Note

- You may check your bank\-registered email for a confirmation on the successful status of your application submission. Learn more about how to [Update Email Address](general-profile-update-email-address.html).

*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*

- Insert your ATM/Debit/Credit Card and key in your **PIN**.
- Select **More Services** and your preferred **Language**.
- Select **ESA\-IPO / Rights Appln/ Bonds /SSB/SGS/Investments**.
- Select **SGS / Singapore Savings Bonds**, followed by **Singapore Savings Bond Redemption**.
- Confirm the on\-screen **T\&Cs** and select the **Bond** that you wish to apply for.
- Key in the **Year and Month** of Bond issuance. Press **Enter** to proceed.
- Select your **Bond** and confirm the on\-screen **T\&Cs**.
- Confirm your **CDP Account Number** and select **Yes** to proceed.
- Key in the **Redemption Amount** and press **Enter**.
- Check your **Redemption Details** and select **Yes** to continue.
- Select your **Debiting Account** for deduction of the S$2 non\-refundable transaction fee.
- Confirm your **Redemption Details** and select **Confirm** to submit your redemption.

### More information

- Redemption can be done in any month before the bond matures with no penalty, and the minimum redemption amount for each Savings Bond issue is $500 (and in multiples of $500 up to the amount you have invested).
- Your cash (along with any accrued interest) will be credited to the designated bank account that is linked to your individual CDP Securities account by the second business day of the following month.
