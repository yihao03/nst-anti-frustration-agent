## Cash out an EDP/EDP\+

Learn how to cash out an EDP/EDP\+ that you have received. If your sender has set a future effective date for the EDP/EDP\+, learn how to set up Auto Cash Out via digibank mobile.

### Important information

- Processing time: Funds will be **credited within 2 working days** upon cashing out an EDP/EDP\+
- EDP/EDP\+ can only be cashed out if it has met the effective date (earliest date to cash out).
