**SMS Q Service**  
If you are visiting any of our DBS/POSB branches, plan your arrival accordingly with our SMS Q service. Please note that the issuance of queue numbers may end earlier depending on the queue situation. Alternatively, visit our 24/7 digital lobbies or bank from the comfort of your home with DBS/POSB digibank. Thank you for your understanding.

 

**New Coin Deposit Machine**  
Please be informed that we have replaced our existing Coin Deposit Machines with a new model that allows you to deposit coins and notes in a single transaction. The new machine is paired together with an ATM and you may begin your coin deposit journey by either inserting a card/passbook into the ATM.
