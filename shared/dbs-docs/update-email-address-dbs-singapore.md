## Update Email Address

Keep your email address updated to receive your transaction alerts, so you are notified of your account activities.

### How to update your email address

There are various channels which you may update your email address with us. The most convenient method would be via **digibank mobile**.
 

##### Get the latest DBS digibank mobile app now!

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

##### Not yet a digibank user?

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
*For customers with ATM/Debit/Credit Card*


 Do ensure that your email address is updated on Singpass first.  

 If you do not have Singpass, register [online](https://www.singpass.gov.sg/home/ui/register/instructions?pid=sg-dbs-help-support-general-profile-update-email-address.html). You can refer to the [Singpass Instructional Guide](https://www.singpass.gov.sg/home/ui/assets/pdf/Singpass_Registration_Guide.pdf?pid=sg-dbs-help-support-general-profile-update-email-address.html).
