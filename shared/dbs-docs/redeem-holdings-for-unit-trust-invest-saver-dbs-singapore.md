## Redeem Holdings for Unit Trust/ Invest\-Saver

Learn how to redeem your Unit Trust/ Invest\-Saver holdings easily via digibank.

### How to redeem holdings for Unit Trust/ Invest\-Saver

There are various channels which you may redeem holdings for Unit Trust/ Invest\-Saver with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

##### Get the latest digibank mobile app now!

- [Log in to digibank online](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/posb/) with your **User ID** and **PIN**.
- Complete the **Authentication Process**.
- Under **Invest**, click on **More Investment Services**.
- Under **Manage Investments**, click on **Redeem Unit Trust or Exchange Traded Fund (ETF)**.
- Select your **Investment Account** from the dropdown and click **Search**.
- Click on **Sell** for the ETF/ Unit Trust plan that you wish to redeem for.
 - Enter the **number of units to redeem**, select the **Crediting Account** and click **Next**.
- Verify the redemption details and click **Submit** to submit your redemption request.

### More information

- Minimum redemption and minimum holding amount varies from unit trust to unit trust. Refer to the respective unit trust's Prospectus and Product Highlights Sheet for more details.
- Minimum holding amount for Exchange Traded Funds is S$100\. Redemption of Exchange Traded Funds on digibank online is applicable for Exchange Traded Funds purchased from DBS in cash only and held by you in a singly basis.
- Redemption proceeds will usually be credited to your designated account within 7 business days from the submission of your redemption request on digibank online.
- A confirmation advice will be sent to you to confirm the amount of your redemption proceeds.
