## Increase DBS Vickers Cash Trading Account Limits

Increase your DBS Vickers cash trading account limits by furnishing us with your latest income documents.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### Important information

- For Dormant Account Reactivation, you may follow the steps under [Request for a credit Review](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/investment-vickers-increase-trading-limits.html#credit-review)
