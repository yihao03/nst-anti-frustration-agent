### Trading: Mainland China A Share Market (SHSZ via Stock Connect)


 Shenzhen/Shanghai\-Hong Kong Stock Connect is a joint project between the Shenzhen Stock Exchange (SZSE)/Shanghai Stock Exchange (SSE) and The Stock Exchange of Hong Kong Limited (SEHK). It allows investors to trade cross\-border into specific stocks listed in the other market. Additionally, it provides overseas investors, including OET clients, with the opportunity to trade China A\-shares listed on the SZSE and SSE.
 


 Trading is conducted on Monday to Friday (excluding non\-Northbound Trading days) at the following times:
 


 Please ensure that you have a valid BCAN set up before trading in the Mainland China A Share market. Should you require assistance, please reach out to your Relationship Manager.

All orders for Mainland China SHSZ A Shares must be within the set price limits. Orders outside these limits will be rejected. For more details, see the rules [here](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/common-disclaimer.page?url=https://www.hkex.com.hk/Mutual-Market/Stock-Connect/Reference-Materials/Rules-and-Regulations&3rdparty=%27HKEX%27). 

**Note:**
- ST stocks refer to stocks that have been resumed from suspension for listing; or stocks that have been relisted; or stocks that have suffered from other significant risks.
- \*ST stocks refer to stocks with the risks of being delisted.

 Northbound trading is only available when both the Hong Kong and Mainland China markets are open. If the Hong Kong market operates on a half\-day trading schedule, Northbound trading will remain open.
