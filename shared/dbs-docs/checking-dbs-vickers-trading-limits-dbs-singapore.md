## Checking DBS Vickers Trading Limits

Check your trading limits before placing any trades to ensure a smooth trading experience.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### Important information

- Your trading limit is also known as Buying Power. It has a buy limit and a sell limit.
- To increase your trading limits click [here](investment-vickers-increase-trading-limits.html).

### How to check DBS Vickers trading limits

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
