## Remove Card from Mobile Wallet

Remove credit and debit cards from your mobile wallet easily in a few steps.

### How to remove card from Mobile Wallet

- Go to your **Apple Wallet App** and select the card you wish to remove and tap on  **icon** on the top right.  

Alternatively, go to **Settings \> Wallet \& Apple Pay \> Select the Card** you wish to remove.
- Tap on **Card Details**.
 - Scroll to the bottom and tap **Remove Card**.
- Tap **Remove** to confirm.

- Go to your **Google Pay App**.
- Tap on your **Profile** on the top right.
- Under **Payment methods**, tap on **Debit/credit card**.
- Select the Credit / Debit Card you wish to remove.
- Tap on the  **icon** on the top right and select **Remove payment method**.
- Tap **Remove** to confirm the request.

- Launch your **Samsung Wallet**  and tap on **All** at the bottom.
- Under **My wallet**,tap on **Payment cards**.
- Tap the card you wish to remove.
- Tap on the  **icon** on the top right and select **Delete Card**.
- Tap **Delete** to confirm the requests.

### More information

- Removing a credit or debit card will also remove its transaction history.
- An active internet connection is required to remove a payment card.
