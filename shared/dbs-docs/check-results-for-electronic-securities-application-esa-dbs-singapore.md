## Check Results for Electronic Securities Application (ESA)

Learn how to check the results of your Electronic Securities Application (ESA) easily using digibank.

### Important information

- For unsuccessful or partially successful applications, monies will be refunded automatically to you into your DBS/POSB account by the third working day after the electronic balloting.

### How to check results for Electronic Securities Application (ESA)

There are various channels which you may check results for your Electronic Securities Application (ESA) with us. The most convenient method would be via digibank mobile.

- Log in to digibank mobile with your **Touch / Face ID** or **digibank User ID \& PIN**.
- Tap on **digiWealth** at the bottom navigation.
- Under **Invest**, tap on **Show all** and select **ESA**.
- Tap on **Your applications** to view Electronic Securities Application (ESA) result.

**Don't have digibank?**  

Get the latest digibank mobile App now!

- [Log in to digibank online](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/) with your **User ID** \& **PIN**.
- Complete the **authentication process**.
- Click on **digiwealth** on the left menu and select **Invest**. Under **Product and services** on the right, click on **ESA**.
- Click on **Your applications** to view Electronic Securities Application (ESA) result.
