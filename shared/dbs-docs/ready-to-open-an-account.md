#### PROOF OF EMPLOYMENT/EDUCATION

Provide the latest copy of either

- Employment/Student pass or
- Dependent/Long\-Term Visit pass or
- In\-Principle Approval (IPA) issued by Ministry of Manpower/ICA or
- Acceptance letter from school

  
1. Name and Foreign Identification Number (FIN) details on document proof should match that on your application form.
2. Document proof must be valid e.g still within 3
 months before the date of application.
3. Signatures and authority stamps should be
 captured clearly, if applicable.

#### PROOF OF RESIDENTIAL ADDRESS

Provide the latest copy of either

- Letter of offer/employment or
- Local utility/telecommunication bill or
- In\-Principle Approval (IPA) issued by Ministry of Manpower/ICA or
- Work permit of foreign domestic worker issue by Ministry of Manpower.

  
1. Name and address should match that on your
 application form.
2. Document must be dated within 3 months before the
 date of application.
3. Signatures and authority stamps should be
 captured clearly, if applicable.
