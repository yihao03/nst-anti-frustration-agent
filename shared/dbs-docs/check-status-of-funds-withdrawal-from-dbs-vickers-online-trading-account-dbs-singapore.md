## Check Status of Funds Withdrawal from DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

Check the status of your funds withdrawal easily via the Online Funds Withdrawal page.

*Part of: Guides \>* [Your Guide to DBS Vickers Online Trading Account](guide-vickers.html)

### Important information

- Cancellation of funds withdrawal request can only be done if the status is pending.   
Cancellation is **not allowed** for processed requests.

### How to check status of funds withdrawal from DBS Vickers Online Trading Account

- [Log in to digibank online](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/) with your **User ID \& PIN**.
- Complete the **Authentication Process**.
- Click on Invest and then select **DBS Vickers Online Trading Account**.
- Click on **Next** and proceed to access DBS Vickers Securities platform.
- Under **My Account**, select **Account Management**.
- Under **Online Instructions**, select **Online Funds Withdrawal**.
- The status of your funds withdrawal request will be shown under **Withdrawal Status**.
 For requests that are already processed, you may view the status at the **Account Activity** page instead.
