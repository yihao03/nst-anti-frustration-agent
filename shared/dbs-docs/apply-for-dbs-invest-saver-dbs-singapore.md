## Apply for DBS Invest\-Saver

Learn how to apply for DBS Invest\-Saver easily via digibank online.

### Who is Eligible?


 You can set up a DBS Invest\-Saver plan if you are:
 - 18 years old and above on the date of application
- Not a US Person
- Have a DBS/POSB Deposit Account with us. Learn more on how to [Open an Account with DBS/POSB](bank-account-new-opening.html).

### How to apply for DBS Invest\-Saver

- Log in to digibank mobile with your **Touch / Face ID** or **digibank User ID \& PIN**.
- Tap on **digiWealth** at the bottom navigation and select **Funds \& ETFs**.
- Under **Explore by categories**, tap on **Show all** and select **ETF Invest\-Saver**.
- Select the ETF of your choice and tap on **Buy**.
- Fill in the necessary details and click **Submit** to confirm your application.
