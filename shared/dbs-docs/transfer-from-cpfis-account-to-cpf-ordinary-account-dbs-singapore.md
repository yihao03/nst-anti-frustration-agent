## Transfer from CPFIS Account to CPF Ordinary Account

Perform a partial or full funds transfer to your CPF Ordinary account easily using digibank online.

### Important information

- There are no service charges for this request.
- There is no minimum and maximum limit to the transfer amount, and you can transfer up to the available balance in your CPFIS account.
- It will take 3 business days for the funds to be transferred to the CPF Ordinary account.
- Cash balances in CPF Investment Account will automatically be credited back to CPF Ordinary Account at the end of the month if there are no investment transaction for 2 consecutive months.

### How to transfer from CPFIS Account to CPF Ordinary Account

##### Already a digibank user?

[Login now](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/)
Forgot [User ID](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-userid) or [PIN](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/digi-web/ngib/forgot-pin)

[Get Started](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/hoap/registration?brand=dbs&entry=IBNextgen)
