## How to redeem SAFRA$

 **Instant redemption**- Offset your purchases instantly at all SAFRA Clubhouses and their [participating merchants](https://www.dbs.com.sg/iwov-resources/media/pdf/cards/participating-merchants.pdf)

**Redeem as cash credits**  
- Log into [digibank online](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/IB/Welcome)
- Redeem SAFRA$ as cash credits to offset your credit card bill

**Redeem as miles**  
- Log into [digibank online](https://internet-banking.dbs.com.sg/IB/Welcome)
- Redeem SAFRA$ as KrisFlyer miles, in blocks of 10,000 KrisFlyer miles
- Every SAFRA$245 can be converted to 10,000 KrisFlyer miles
- An administrative fee of S$27\.25 (inclusive of GST) applies for each conversion
