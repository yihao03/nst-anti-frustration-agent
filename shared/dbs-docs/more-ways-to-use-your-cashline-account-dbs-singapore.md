DBS Cashline## Transact like a regular bank account. Withdrawals, transfers, bill payments and more, online or at an ATM.

## Ways to use DBS Cashline

### Online Fund Transfer or FAST

- Use Cashline to perform online fund transfer or FAST to third payment account.
- Select Transfer \> Choose transfer options \> Choose Cashline as the account to transfer.
### Transfer to your own DBS/POSB savings account

- Transfer money from your Cashline to your desired DBS/POSB savings account with step by step guide below.
- To transfer to other account(s), simply select from the list under the Transfer Tab.
## 

 digibank Online**Step 1**: Log in to your **digibank online**.

**Step 2**: Under **Pay and Transfer**, select **Local Transfer**.

**Step 3**: Select your **Cashline account** and **choose a recipient**.Enter the **amount** you wish to transfer and click **Next**.

**Step 4**: Review your transfer details and click **Submit**.

 digibank Mobile**Step 1**: Log in to your **digibank app**.

**Step 2**: Tap **Pay and Transfer**, choose **Local Transfer**.

**Step 3**: Select your **Cashline account** and **choose a recipient**.Enter the **amount** you wish to transfer and tap **Next**.

**Step 4**: Review your transfer details and tap **Submit**.

### Online bill payment

Use DBS Cashline to pay your bills via digibank. Refer to step by step guide below.

## 

 digibank Online**Step 1**: Log in to your **digibank online**.

**Step 2**: Under **Pay and Transfer**, select **Pay bills \& cards**.

**Step 3**: Under **Bills** **Tab,** choose the **billing organisation** in your pre\-arranged list and enter amount to pay, click **Next**.  
(If the Billing Organisation is not in your pre\-arranged list, follow the steps in [Add Bill Payment Organisation](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-payment-add-bill-payment-organisations.html?pid=sg-dbs-pweb-borrow-sg-cle16-txtlink-bank-payment-add-bill-payment-organisations#digibank-online).)

**Step 4**: Review your payment details and click **Submit**.

 digibank Mobile**Step 1**: Log in to your **digibank app**.

**Step 2**: Tap **Pay and Transfer** at bottom navigation bar, choose **Pay bills \& cards**.

**Step 3**: Under **Bills** **Tab,** choose the **billing organisation** in your pre\-arranged list and enter amount to pay, tap **Next**.  
(If the Billing Organisation is not in your pre\-arranged list, follow the steps in [Add Bill Payment Organisation](https://www.dbs.com.sg/personal/support/bank-payment-add-bill-payment-organisations.html?pid=sg-dbs-pweb-borrow-sg-cle16-txtlink2-bank-payment-add-bill-payment-organisations).)

**Step 4**: Review your transfer details and tap **Submit**.

 Other Bill Payment Channels- ATM \- Selected Billing Organisation only.
- AXS \- Selected Billing Organisation only.
## ATM withdrawal

### Withdraw cash from your DBS Cashline at any DBS/POSB ATM

Upon approval of your DBS Cashline, you can start to withdraw cash from the account with your ATM card. Simply select the DBS Cashline account number to withdraw cash!

### Perform NETS transaction

You can also use the account to pay for purchases with the ATM card with DBS Cashline as your primary account.
