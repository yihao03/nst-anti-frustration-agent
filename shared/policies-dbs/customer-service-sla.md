# Customer Service SLA & Support Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-01

## Response Times

| Channel        | Priority | First Response | Resolution Target |
|----------------|----------|----------------|-------------------|
| Phone          | Critical | < 15 minutes   | 2 hours           |
| Phone          | High     | < 30 minutes   | 8 hours           |
| Email/Chat     | Critical | < 1 hour       | 4 hours           |
| Email/Chat     | High     | < 2 hours      | 24 hours          |
| Email/Chat     | Normal   | < 4 hours      | 48 hours          |
| Branch Visit   | All      | Immediate      | Same day          |

## Priority Definitions

- **Critical**: Account compromised, unauthorized transactions, card lost/stolen, fraud suspected, online banking access blocked
- **High**: Transaction disputes, failed transfers, payment processing errors, significant account discrepancies
- **Normal**: Account inquiries, statement requests, general banking questions, product information
- **Low**: Feedback, suggestions, documentation requests, general inquiries

## Support Scope

We provide support for:
- Account opening and management
- Online and mobile banking assistance
- Card services (activation, blocking, replacement)
- Transaction inquiries and disputes
- Loan and credit product support
- Investment and wealth management inquiries
- Bill payments and fund transfers

We do not provide support for:
- Financial or investment advice (unless through licensed advisors)
- Third-party merchant disputes beyond transaction processing
- Issues caused by customer negligence or fraud

## Escalation Process

1. **Tier 1 (Customer Service Representative)**: Handles general inquiries, account information, basic transactions, card services
2. **Tier 2 (Specialist Team)**: Escalated for complex disputes, fraud investigation, loan issues, technical banking problems
3. **Tier 3 (Branch Manager/Compliance)**: Escalated for serious complaints, regulatory issues, significant financial disputes, legal matters

## When to Escalate

Escalate to a senior agent or branch manager when:
- The customer explicitly requests to speak with a manager
- The issue involves suspected fraud or account compromise
- The customer expresses strong dissatisfaction (3+ interactions)
- The issue involves potential regulatory or compliance concerns
- The dispute exceeds standard resolution authority limits

## Digital vs Branch Banking Support

**Digital Banking (Online/Mobile)**:
- 24/7 support via phone and chat
- Email support for non-urgent matters
- Self-service options through mobile app and online portal

**Branch Banking**:
- In-person assistance during business hours
- Complex transactions requiring physical documentation
- Notary and certification services

## Customer Satisfaction

- Follow up within 24 hours of resolution for Critical and High priority cases
- Document all complaints for regulatory reporting and monthly review
- Continuously improve based on customer feedback and service metrics

## SLA Exclusions

SLA targets do not apply during:
- Scheduled system maintenance (announced in advance)
- Force majeure events
- Issues caused by third-party payment networks
- Circumstances beyond our reasonable control
- Public holidays
